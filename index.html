From one collector to another

Built because nothing else fit.

I've been collecting 16mm film for years. Prints stacked in cans, reels spread across shelves, a mental inventory that only works until it doesn't.

I needed to know what I had, where it was, who borrowed it — all from my phone while standing in front of a pile of cans at an estate sale. Nothing I found was built for collectors. So I built VAULT.

Your data lives in Google Sheets. No subscription, no server I'm running that disappears. Just a spreadsheet you own and an app that reads it.

Setup in minutes

Four steps to your archive.

01
Fork the repo
Fork this repo on GitHub and enable Pages. Your archive is live in seconds.
02
Copy the Sheet
Copy the VAULT template to your Google Drive. Your data, your spreadsheet.
03
Get API keys
Free keys from TMDB and OMDB for automatic metadata and cover art.
04
Launch
Open your app, run the setup wizard, and start scanning your collection.
